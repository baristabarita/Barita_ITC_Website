New Characters Announced - Shenhe and Yun Jin
Date Posted Nov 24, 2021

Two new characters are coming to Genshin Impact, Shenhe and Yun Jin, as part of the 2.4 update scheduled
to release in 2022.


 

〓First look at "Lonesome Transcendence" Shenhe〓
Shenhe “comes from a branch family of a clan of exorcists and due to certain
reasons, the Cloud Retainer Adepti took her in as a disciple, as she stands out as special from
amongst the mortals she has instructed.”

Thanks to this, Shenhe has a unique constitution, an immensely strong will, and a great talent for
adepti arts, all of which have won her their acknowledgement.

 

〓First look at “Stage Lucida” Yun Jin.〓
Yun Jin is a famous figure in Liyue Harbor's opera scene, also known for her sweet singing voice
and intricate costumes, as well as her animated and dynamic performances. Whether it's a delicate
and dignified young lady or a mighty and honourable heroine, she is capable of playing any role.

Besides, what's even rarer is that she is also a playwright, as almost all of the Yun-Han Opera Troupe
new plays have been written by Yun Jin, always displaying a dignified and elegant image.
