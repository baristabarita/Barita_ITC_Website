Return to Liyue - Lantern Rite 2 Coming Soon
Date Posted Nov 24, 2021

Genshin Impact's 2.4 update will bring back two of the most beloved events to the game
with the re-run of the infamous Lantern Rite festival of Liyue.


We've already taken a look at the new characters, weapons, and banners coming to Genshin Impact,
so now it's time to check the events this big update will bring with it.

 

〓CELEBRATE THE LANTERN RITE〓
As always, a crucial pillar of content for each update comes in the form of featured events, however,
for the first time since the game’s release, all players will have the chance to enjoy a couple of the
game's most popular events once again with the return of the Lantern Rite festival.

Lantern Rite 2 in Beta

For Genshin Impact veterans, the Lantern Rite festival stands among the most beloved events of the
entire game, as players ventured into a series of quests to help the powerful adepti Xiao deal with
some bad spirits, while the Traveller and Paimon tried to make him join the celebrations in Liyue.

 

According to the event banner found in the beta, it seems both Ganyu and the newcomer Shenhe
will also take part in this year’s Lantern Rite quests, so we can expect a couple of changes and more
exclusive rewards to come.
